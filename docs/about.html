Organizers & Sponsors

2025 Symposium Organizers

West Coast Neuroecon 2025 is organized by:

Ian Krajbich

University of California, Los Angeles

Dr. Ian Krajbich is a leading researcher in neuroeconomics and decision neuroscience. His work focuses on understanding the computational and neural mechanisms underlying value-based decision-making, using a combination of behavioral experiments, computational modeling, and neuroimaging techniques.

Research Interests: - Value-based decision making - Computational modeling of choice - Visual attention and choice - Food and consumer decisions

Kianté Fernandez

University of California, Los Angeles

Dr. Kianté Fernandez conducts research at the intersection of neuroscience, economics, and decision-making. Their work contributes to our understanding of how neural systems support economic choices and decision processes.

Research Interests: - Neuroeconomics - Decision neuroscience - Cognitive neuroscience

Sponsors & Support

We welcome sponsorship support for West Coast Neuroecon 2025. Your sponsorship helps make this important academic gathering possible and supports the neuroeconomics research community.

Become a Sponsor

We are currently seeking sponsors for the 2025 symposium. Sponsorship opportunities are available at various levels.

To discuss sponsorship options, please contact us at wcneuroecon@gmail.com.

Why Sponsor?

Supporting West Coast Neuroecon provides:

  • Visibility: Exposure to leading researchers and academics in neuroeconomics
  • Community Support: Direct contribution to advancing scientific knowledge
  • Networking: Opportunities to connect with the research community
  • Recognition: Acknowledgment at the symposium and on our website
  • Impact: Help support student travel grants and make the symposium accessible

Sponsorship Levels

We offer several sponsorship tiers to fit different budgets and goals:

Platinum Sponsor - Benefits include: - Prominent logo placement on website and symposium materials - Acknowledgment in opening and closing remarks - Opportunity for brief remarks at symposium - Recognition in program materials - Social media recognition - 2 complimentary registrations

Gold Sponsor - Benefits include: - Logo placement on website and symposium materials - Acknowledgment in opening remarks - Recognition in program materials - Social media recognition - 1 complimentary registration

Silver Sponsor - Benefits include: - Logo placement on website - Recognition in program materials - Social media recognition

Bronze Sponsor - Benefits include: - Name listing on website - Recognition in program materials

In-Kind Sponsorship - We also welcome in-kind donations such as: - Coffee and catering services - Printing services - Technology and AV support - Venue support

Acknowledgments

Major Support

The Luskin Lecture for Thought Leadership

We are proud to acknowledge the generous support of The Luskin Lecture for Thought Leadership at UCLA College.

The Luskin Lecture for Thought Leadership was established in 2011 as part of a transformative gift from Meyer and Renee Luskin. The lecture series has featured distinguished speakers including President Bill Clinton, Kofi Annan, Hillary Rodham Clinton, and other world leaders, giving UCLA College an opportunity to share knowledge and expand the dialogue among scholars, leaders in government and business, and the greater Los Angeles community.

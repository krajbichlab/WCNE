Who Should Register?

The symposium welcomes decision scientists from across disciplines:

  • Faculty and Researchers: Academic researchers working in neuroeconomics, decision neuroscience, and related fields
  • Graduate Students: PhD students conducting research in relevant areas
  • Postdoctoral Researchers: Postdocs interested in neuroeconomics and decision science
  • West Coast Researchers: Participants from institutions across the West Coast
  • Los Angeles Area Researchers: Colleagues from Caltech, USC, UCLA, and other LA institutions
  • Industry Professionals: Practitioners in decision science and behavioral economics

About the Venue

The Meyer & Renee Luskin Conference Center is a world-class conference facility located in the heart of UCLA’s campus. This LEED Platinum-certified building exemplifies sustainability while providing state-of-the-art facilities for academic gatherings.

Venue Highlights:

  • 25,000 square feet of multi-purpose meeting and event space
  • LEED Platinum certified for sustainable design
  • 254 guest rooms with luxurious amenities
  • On-site dining at Plateia restaurant (Mediterranean-inspired, California-fresh)
  • State-of-the-art facilities for conferences and symposia
  • Located on UCLA campus in the Westwood neighborhood of Los Angeles

Facilities & Amenities:

  • Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi connected to UCLA Campus Backbone Network
  • Well-equipped fitness center
  • Business center
  • Professional on-site catering services
  • 24-hour front desk
  • Convenient access to UCLA facilities and resources

Getting to UCLA

By Air

Los Angeles International Airport (LAX) - Approximately 13 miles from UCLA campus - Major international airport with connections worldwide - Ground transportation options include: - Ride-share services (Uber, Lyft) - Taxi services - Rental cars - Public transportation via Metro

Alternative Airports: - Burbank Bob Hope Airport (BUR) - About 20 miles - Long Beach Airport (LGB) - About 30 miles - John Wayne Airport (SNA) - About 45 miles

By Car

UCLA is located in Westwood, easily accessible via: - I-405 Freeway (exit Wilshire Blvd or Sunset Blvd) - Sunset Boulevard - Wilshire Boulevard

Parking: Campus parking information and visitor permits will be provided to registered attendees.
